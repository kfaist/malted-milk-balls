About

This is an interactive experience where your camera feed is processed in real-time through TouchDesigner and sent back to you as an abstracted artistic interpretation.

Your Camera (OUT): Your live video and audio are sent to the TouchDesigner processing system.

Transformed (IN): The processed, abstracted version streams back to you in real-time.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Allow camera and microphone access
  • Step 2: Your feed streams to TouchDesigner for live processing
  • Step 3: Watch your transformed self appear in the right panel
  • Technology: LiveKit WebRTC + TouchDesigner + Real-time Processing
